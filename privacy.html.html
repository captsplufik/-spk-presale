SPK Token Presale Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with our Presale:

  • Wallet Address: We store your TON wallet address for transaction processing
  • Transaction Data: We record contribution amounts and timestamps
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information (for security purposes)

2. How We Use Information

Your data is used to:

  • Process Presale contributions
  • Distribute SPK tokens
  • Prevent fraud and ensure compliance
  • Improve our services

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Limited sharing occurs with:

  • TON blockchain (public ledger)
  • Legal authorities (if required by law)
  • Service providers (only as necessary for Presale operations)

4. Data Retention

We retain:

  • Transaction records indefinitely (for audit purposes)
  • Other data for 3 years after Presale completion

5. Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your data
  • Withdraw consent (though this may affect Presale participation)
  • Request deletion of non-essential data

6. Security Measures

We implement:

  • SSL encryption
  • Smart contract audits
  • Regular security reviews

7. Contact Us

For privacy concerns: privacy@spktoken.com
